Biography

Academic Profile

Dr. Hany El-Ghaish is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computers and Control Engineering at the Faculty of Engineering, Tanta University, Egypt. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST) in 2019, with a dissertation on multi-modal human action recognition using deep learning, under the supervision of international advisors from Egypt and Japan.

His research interests encompass deep learning, computer vision, medical AI, cyber-physical system security, and intelligent control. He has authored and co-authored over 17 peer-reviewed articles in Web of Science-indexed journals, including Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence, Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, IEEE Access, and Neural Computing and Applications.

Dr. El-Ghaish actively supervises graduate researchers at Tanta University at both the M.Sc. and Ph.D. levels, and has led multiple award-winning student graduation projects in the fields of artificial intelligence and embedded systems. He maintains active international research collaborations with partners in Saudi Arabia and Singapore.
